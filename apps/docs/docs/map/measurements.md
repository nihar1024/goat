# Measurements

The **Measurements** tool lets you measure distances, areas, and routes directly on the map. Click the <img src={require('/img/icons/ruler-horizontal.png').default} alt="Measurements" style={{ maxHeight: "20px", maxWidth: "20px", objectFit: "cover"}}/> **Measurements** button in the map navigation controls to open the tool panel.

## Measurement types

| Tool | Measures | How to draw |
|---|---|---|
| **Line** | Path length along a drawn line | Click to add points. Double-click to finish. |
| **Polygon** | Area and perimeter of a drawn polygon | Click to add points. Double-click or click the starting point to finish. |
| **Circle** | Radius, area, and perimeter of a circle | Click and drag to draw a circle. |
| **Flight Distance** | Straight-line (geodesic) distance between points | Click to draw a flight path. Double-click to finish. |
| **Walking** | Routed walking distance and travel time | Click to add waypoints. Double-click to finish. |
| **Car** | Routed car distance and travel time | Click to add waypoints. Double-click to finish. |

## Snap to layers

Enable **Snap to layers** to snap your drawing to vertices and edges of existing map layers. A red ring indicator appears when a snap target is detected.

## Managing measurements

- Click any measurement on the map to select it and edit its vertices.
- To delete a single measurement, select it and click the delete icon in the panel.
- To remove all measurements, click **Close Measure Tools**.
