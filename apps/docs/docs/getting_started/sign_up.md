# Sign Up

Ready to start with GOAT? Follow these simple steps to create your account and start exploring the world of geospatial analysis:

<div class="step">
  <div class="step-number">1</div>
  <div class="content">Go to the <a href="https://goat.plan4better.de/home">GOAT login page</a>, click on <code>Create an account</code>, fill in your details and click the <code>Get started!</code> button to access the registration form.</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
  <div class="step-number">2</div>
  <div class="content">Check your email inbox for the confirmation email from GOAT.</div>
</div>

<div class="step">
  <div class="step-number">3</div>
  <div class="content">You are ready! Sign in and start using GOAT.</div>
</div>

## Troubleshooting

### Forgot Password?
If you forget your password, click on the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

### Didn’t Receive the Verification Email?
Check your spam folder. If you still can’t find the email, contact our support team at [support@plan4better.de](mailto:support@plan4better.de).

### Need Help?
If you encounter any issues during the sign-up process, our support team is here to help. Contact us at [support@plan4better.de](mailto:support@plan4better.de).
